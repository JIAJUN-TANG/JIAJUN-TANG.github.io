# About Me
I'm now studying in [Nanjing University](https://www.nju.edu.cn/en/) as a postgraduate student. I got a B.A. in [Central South University](https://en.csu.edu.cn). At the same time, I was also a student member in the Center for Intelligent Media and Communication Research leading by my tutor.
